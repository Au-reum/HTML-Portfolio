My Favorite movies of all time

Angela's top 3 movies

1. Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron

2002 ‧ Family/Western ‧ 1h 24m

This has always been my favorite movie since I was young, this might be my most watched movie as well. I just really like horses, their resilience is inspiring. The culture that is represented in the movie is quite interesting as well. I was invested back then, not as much today but it will remain as my top 1 among anything else.

2. A Christmas Carol

2009 ‧ Family/Fantasy ‧ 1h 36m

Do I really have to explain myself with this one? this might be the best christmas movie out there in a long time. It's generational, the animation, the theme, the story, everything is great in this movie. Of course it has its flaws but the positives are greater than the negatives in this case.

3. Mickey's Once Upon a Christmas

1999 ‧ Family/Comedy ‧ 1h 10m

I don't really remember anything about the movie but for some reason the 3D animation stuck to me when I was a kid. That's all I really remember in this movie and yeah it's about christmas stuff.
